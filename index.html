About Elixir

Welcome to Tiwana Automobiles! Your one-stop solution for all European cars. We deal with BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Porsche, Rolls-Royce, Bentley, and more. Our expert team delivers dealer-standard repairs at cost-effective prices.

Tiwana Automobiles is a proprietorship firm offering multibrand luxury car service, maintenance, body-shop, and paint-shop repairs in Chandigarh. Completing 7 years of operation in 2024, the workshop is led by Mr. Surender Singh Tiwana, a respected industry leader.

We proudly handle repairs for brands such as BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Porsche, and Volvo, at our aftersales facility in Chandigarh. Expansion plans are underway to serve more automobile brands.

To guarantee top-notch service, our team has undergone specialized training in aftersales, business systems, spare parts, and customer service.

Vehicles come to the workshop from multiple locations across Punjab, Jammu and Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, and Haryana, as customers are delighted with the provided services and experience. Managed by an experienced team, Tiwana Automobiles is set to provide the best customer experience in the future.
